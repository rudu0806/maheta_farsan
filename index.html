Three Generations of Excellence

Since 1975, Mehta Farsan has been serving the finest traditional Gujarati snacks and sweets. What started as a small family shop has grown into a beloved local institution.

We use only the finest ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Every item is made fresh daily with the same care and attention to detail that made us famous.

Our Promise: Quality ingredients, traditional methods, and uncompromising taste in every bite.
